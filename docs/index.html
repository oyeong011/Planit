Deep Dive

Built different.

Hermes Memory

An AI that knows your patterns

Most AI assistants forget everything after a session. Hermes is different — it extracts long-term insights from your conversations and applies them automatically.

  • Detects preferred focus blocks and energy cycles
  • Remembers recurring context ("every Mon is standup")
  • Applies insights silently to future scheduling
AI Chat

Just talk to your calendar

Type in plain language. Calen understands what you mean and takes action — creating, moving, or restructuring your day.

  • "Move my 3pm to tomorrow morning"
  • "Fill my afternoon with deep work blocks"
  • "What's free this week for a 2-hour focus session?"
Review Tab

Know how you spend your time

Weekly stats, habit streaks, and completion rates — the Review tab turns your calendar into a personal productivity dashboard.

  • 7-day completion bar chart
  • Year-long habit grass grid
  • AI-generated weekly retrospective

Setup

Up in 3 minutes.

No configuration files. No signup. Just install and connect.

STEP 01

Install via Homebrew or .dmg

Download the universal binary or use Homebrew Cask. Signed and notarized — no Gatekeeper warnings.

$ brew tap oyeong011/calen
$ brew install --cask calen-ai
STEP 02

Connect your Google Calendar

One-click OAuth flow. Calen only asks for the scopes it needs. Your credentials stay in your Keychain.

# Scopes used:
calendar.events
calendar.readonly
STEP 03

Add your AI key & start chatting

Paste your Claude or OpenAI API key in Settings. Then ask Calen anything — it handles the rest.

# Settings → AI Provider
Claude (Anthropic) ✓
OpenAI / Codex ✓
